The Founder
Angelique
Klimis.
Founder & Principal Consultant

RCRUIT was built on a simple but non-negotiable belief: the right hire changes everything. Not just the role — the team dynamic, the culture, the growth trajectory of the whole business.

With a background in Law and Psychology and years building and leading high-performing teams across the health and wellness sector, Angelique brings something most recruiters can't: genuine commercial understanding paired with deep human insight.

For the past several years, she has specialised exclusively in medical and aesthetics recruitment — partnering with leading clinics, growing groups and global organisations to place professionals who don't just fit the job description, they elevate the business.

"I don't just fill roles. I look at how each hire fits into the bigger picture — because that's where the real value is."

Clients work with RCRUIT because they want someone who will challenge them where needed, understand their goals with precision, and genuinely care about building the right team — not just closing the brief.

The RCRUIT Difference

We work for you

01.

We listen first

Before we talk roles, we understand what you actually want. Your goals, your non-negotiables, where you want to be in two years. The brief matters because you matter.

Access the unadvertised

Not every great role goes public. RCRUIT works with leading clinics, groups and global brands — which means you hear about opportunities before they ever go to market.

Real preparation
