What We Offer

Five ways we
can help.

We run the full search from brief to placement — active headhunting, candidate screening, structured assessments, interview management, and offer negotiation. Every search is handled personally, with a six-month guarantee included as standard. You get one consultant, full accountability, and a placement built to last.

Short-term doesn't mean low standards. We apply the same rigour to flexible and project-based hires — covering maternity leave, clinic launches, peak demand, or specific business projects. You receive a thoroughly vetted professional who can contribute from day one.

Most placements fail in the first 90 days — not because of the hire, but because of the handover. As an optional add-on, we build a structured onboarding plan tailored to your new hire and your business. Includes a 30/60/90 day plan, role success metrics, manager guidance, and a retention strategy. We don't just place — we make sure they succeed.

The systems your team works within determine how well your hires perform. We build role-specific SOPs and company-wide process frameworks that create clarity, reduce founder dependency, and give new hires the structure to hit the ground running. We build the systems that make your hires perform.

Strategic people support without the cost of a full-time HR hire. We work with founders and directors on hiring strategy, salary benchmarking, team structure, performance guidance, and scaling plans. Available as a project or ongoing retainer. Strategic input, when you need it.
